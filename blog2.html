How Accurate is IP Geolocation?

IP geolocation estimates your location based on your IP address. Accuracy depends on your ISP, region, and whether you use a VPN or proxy.

What Affects Accuracy?

  • Database Quality: Some databases are more up-to-date than others.
  • Network Type: Mobile, corporate, and VPN networks can obscure your real location.
  • Region: Urban areas are usually more accurate than rural ones.

Typical Accuracy

Most users will see city or region-level accuracy. Exact addresses are rarely possible.
